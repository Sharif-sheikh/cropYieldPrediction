  1. Go to the Home page.
  2. You will find two sections:
    • Single Year Prediction: Enter one year (e.g., 2025) and click "Predict" to see the predicted potato yield for that year.
    • Multiple Year Prediction: Enter a start and end year (e.g., 2020 to 2025) and click "Predict Range" to get yield predictions for each year in that range.
  3. Scroll down to view the results in a table format.
  4. You can navigate to the Potatoes Data page to view historical production data.
